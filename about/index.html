The Back-Story Of Evolution

Quadmatics was brought to life by a team of Financial Technology entrepreneurs who had already been ardent advocates in the Self Directed Investment movement. Traders in particular were looking to manage more of their money instead of having other people do it for them for a fee.

Fueled by the 'technology boom of the internet' in the last decade, online trading brokerages allowed participants easy access to the market. People looking at the market were desperate to find good, momentous stocks. But the question is how do you catch them as they happen in real-time? So the founders decided to call the ball, ─ turning it out to the very basic essence of trade forecasting.

Each and everyday we are committed to predict the markets a bit better, and we will not stop until we get it right.
