Founders History Philosophy

Meet The Heart Of Quadmatics

Sandeep Panja and Dipankar Mukherjee, both are active managing partners at Quadmatics, and are closely involved in the day-to-day operations of the firm. Sandeep focuses on the firm’s expansion to new markets and asset classes, and Dipankar on improvement and development of Quadmatics' forecasting algorithms.

As leaders, they provide support and guidance as company grows and new generations of talent takes on more responsibility. All the partners work with the code base on a daily basis.

Back-Drop - How It All Started

The biggest motivation that kept pushing onwards was, Sandeep's self desperation and struggle to come up with a classic price-time determinant for his daily intraday bets. The failures of rationalising a good trade-setup kept him gather enough perseverance to stuck by the nook and look out for solutions with an open mind.

That is how Quadmatics was born. We take delight in working with the most sophisticated technology platforms, on the most alluring projects and problems, in the most exciting areas of finance.

Predicting the market is the guiding principle in everything we do. Each and everyday we are committed to predict it a bit better and we will not stop until we get it right.

Where Best Gets Even Better
