Meet The Heart Of Quadmatics

We are a small team of buy side and sell side traders who thrive on challenges and solving problems. We have built businesses in past, architected SaaS systems, regularly trade multiple asset class, and now build stock apps that serve our needs and hopefully yours.

On How It All Started

The biggest motivation that kept us pushing onwards was our own self desperation and struggle to come up with a classic price-time theorem for daily intraday bets. Discovering explosive moves early almost seemed impossible, and there were no option to anticipate breakouts. The failures of rationalising a good trade-setup gave us enough perseverance to think out of the box and lookout for novel solutions with an open mind.

We always believed there is a sweetspot in every trade that carries the greatest return at the lowest risk. Much like being in a trend or momentum. With this in mind we built Quadmatics - a set of algos to uncover stocks that can make the fastest moves and put on the strongest inertia.

Predicting the market is the guiding principle in everything we do. Each and everyday we are committed to predict it a bit better and we will not stop until we get it right.

Where The Best Gets Even Better

Quadmatics is where bright, young talented minds turn and attack interesting and challenging market problems. The pace is fast and rigorous, collaborative not cutthroat. Decisions are driven by logic and approach. You focus on what you do best. No distractions.

Brilliant minds will push constantly to raise your own bar. We count among us who brings up when we are wrong and talk openly about our mistakes. Staying humble propels us to question our perceived assumptions, and to update our views on new arguments. Decision making is as decentralized as reasonably possible. Any brilliance is supported, celebrated and rewarded.

Breaking Down Barriers

There're times money can be made by just holding on. Times when money can be made by jumping in, and times when money can be made by jumping out. Identifying good stocks is merely part of the story. Bracketing them in the right stage and playing accordingly is the bigger challenge.

We bet on stocks based on a thin-line of distinction between Hope and Hype; when pessimism validates and shows early traction. But pullout before it reaches Hysteria when almost everybody wants to join in. Stock momentums are more meaningful and eventful for us than a new high or low.

Built on the time principles of day trading and long-term investing, Quadmatics allows you to enter the trend before it becomes trend. We power some of the largest financial institutions in India and the Middle-East serving hundreds of investors on a daily basis, helping them receive actionable insights on their investments and savings.
